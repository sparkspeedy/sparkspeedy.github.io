Research

I am a Ph.D. student at the IRIDIA, the Institute for Interdisciplinary Studies on Artificial Intelligence, of the Université Libre de Bruxelles, in Belgium. My study focuses on "Resiliency of collective decision making in robot swarms" under the supervision of Dr. Andreagiovanni Reina and Prof. Marco Dorigo, with a scholarship funded by TRAIL — Walloon Trusted AI Labs — with the ARIAC Project.
